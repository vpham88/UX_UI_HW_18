About Me

I specialize in UX Research and Design with a strong interest in UI Design. I am passionate about improving the lives of others by first understanding user needs and ideating potential solutions. I enjoy solving unique and complex user problems that requires innovative and outside-of-the-box solutions.

I enjoy the ideation phase and brainstorming with a team to come up with a new and fun idea for clients. In a team, I am typically the creative or project lead.

I am also a life-longer learner and I enjoy learning new things and new ways of accomplishing a task which is why I have enjoyed the UX field. It's always evolving and it challenges you to be more creative with each solution.
